Welcome to RunPacer 🏃‍♂️

RunPacer is a web and mobile app that helps runners of all levels stay motivated and progress with structured, personalized training plans. Whether you're a beginner or training for your next marathon, RunPacer adapts to you.

Our Mission

RunPacer exists to make running simpler, smarter, and more enjoyable. We believe that everyone deserves a plan that fits their life, goals, and pace.

  • 🎯 Personalized plans
  • 📈 Track your progress with synced data
  • ⏱️ Seamless integration with Garmin and Strava
